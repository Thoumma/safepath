[EN] Login lockout — after 5 consecutive wrong passwords the lock screen shows too-many-wrong-passwords with a countdown (try again in 0:28). The cooldown starts at 30 seconds, doubles on further failures (capped at 30 minutes) and survives app relaunch. After the countdown the real password logs in normally. Android emulator view (1440×3040).
