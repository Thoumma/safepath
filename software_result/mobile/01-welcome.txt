[EN] Welcome screen — first launch of the SafeZone mobile app for Lao travellers abroad. Introduces the four core functions: the SOS button (sends your GPS position to trusted contacts and the response console over 2 channels), the encrypted passport vault, the optional fake (duress) password that opens a decoy vault and silently alerts for help, and confidential trafficking reporting. The button at the bottom starts setup. Android emulator view (1440×3040).
