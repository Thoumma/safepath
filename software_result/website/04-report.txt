[EN] Report page — the public anonymous report form. Anyone can choose the type of trafficking (forced labour, sexual exploitation, domestic servitude, child trafficking, forced marriage, other), describe what happened, and optionally add country/city/place, a date, up to 3 photos, and contact details — then submit confidentially. An emergency notice points urgent cases to call 191 or 1300 instead. Desktop view (1440×900).
