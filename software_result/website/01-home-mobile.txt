[EN] Home page — the public landing page of SafeZone, an anti-trafficking website for Lao travellers. It shows the headline “People are not goods to be bought and sold”, key statistics on modern slavery, the warning signs of trafficking to watch for, the types of exploitation that can be reported, and buttons leading to the anonymous report form. Mobile view (390×844).
